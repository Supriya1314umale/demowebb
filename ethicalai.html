Ethical AI, guided by human care and clinical responsibility.



We use AI to enhance access, learning, and operational efficiency, while keeping clinical judgment, therapeutic relationships, and decision making fully human-led.

Transparency, privacy, and ethical responsibility guide every use of technology within our organisation.

We do not use AI to diagnose, replace therapists, or interfere with the therapeutic relationship in any way.

Data protection, informed consent, and responsible use are non-negotiable, because technology should reduce barriers to care, not create new ones.
